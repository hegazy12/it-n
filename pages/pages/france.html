France is located in the European continent, and its territory is distributed on many Atlantic and Mediterranean coasts, as it extends from the Mediterranean Sea to the Atlantic Ocean. ABOUT THE CITIES OF FRANCE

Paris

It is the capital of France. It is located on the banks of the Seine River in the northern part of the country.
It has many tourist places, the most important of which are:


1: Eiffel tower

The Eiffel Tower is one of the symbols of Paris,The tower is said to consist of 8,000 metal pieces. Visitors can enjoy dining in the restaurants on the floors of the Eiffel Tower, and the charming view of Paris and its suburbs, as the field of view can extend from the top of the tower to 70 km towards the horizon on clear days, and it is worth noting that the Eiffel Tower is one of the most important attractions for tourists in France ; As it is the most paid tourist place for tourists in the world, it was also nicknamed the Iron Lady because of its agility despite its huge size.

2: Disneyland

Disneyland amusement park opened in central Paris in 1992. It is considered one of the most attractive attractions for tourists in Paris. Disneyland includes many restaurants, hotels, Disney movie galleries, and shopping centers. Disneyland contains 57 rides, one of the most famous of these is the Pirates of the Caribbean game, and there is also the Walt Disney Studios park, which opened in 2002, in which there are games inspired by films.
